# Foresight Training - Quick Fixes Plan

## Status: In Progress

### Issues Found
1. **TypeError**: `model_max_length` not valid in TrainingArguments
2. **Potential issue**: `gradient_checkpointing_kwargs` may conflict
3. **Potential issue**: `ddp_find_unused_parameters` for single GPU

## Fixes Required

### 1. Update config.py training args
Remove invalid parameters from `to_training_args_dict()`:
- Remove `model_max_length` (use `max_seq_length` in SFTTrainer instead)
- Remove `gradient_checkpointing_kwargs` (let transformers handle defaults)
- Remove `ddp_find_unused_parameters` (only for multi-GPU DDP)

### 2. Safe parameters to keep
